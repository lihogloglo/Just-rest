Model and sources

What load means

Load is an internal recovery score for each muscle bucket. It is not kilograms, calories, training stress score, or a medical measurement.

bucket load = sum(durationFactor * intensityFactor * noveltyFactor * bucketWeight * activityMultiplier * decay)
durationFactor = minutes / 60
decay = 0.5 ^ (hoursSinceWorkout / activityHalfLifeHours)

A 60 minute moderate familiar workout with bucket weight 1 and activity multiplier 40 starts at 40 points for that bucket. The half-life controls how quickly those points fade.

Recovery thresholds

  • Green: below 25 points.
  • Caution: 25 to 59 points.
  • Rest: 60 points or more.

"Green in" is the projected time until the bucket decays below 25 points if no new workouts are added.

Data confidence

The activity presets in this file are broad heuristic mappings. wger is a good source for exercise, muscle, and equipment data, but it does not provide the recovery half-lives or load multipliers used here. Those numbers need calibration before this can be treated as validated training science.
